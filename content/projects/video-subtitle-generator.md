## Overview

An AI-powered solution that automatically generates and translates subtitles for videos, supporting multiple languages. This tool makes video content accessible to global audiences by providing accurate, synchronized subtitles in various languages.

## Features

- Automatic speech recognition and transcription
- Multi-language subtitle generation
- Real-time subtitle synchronization
- Support for multiple video formats
- Batch processing capabilities
- Export in various subtitle formats (SRT, VTT, etc.)

## Technologies Used

- AI/ML models for speech recognition
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Translation APIs
- Video processing frameworks

## Use Cases

- Content creators making videos accessible globally
- Educational platforms providing multilingual content
- Corporate training videos with international teams
- Social media content localization
